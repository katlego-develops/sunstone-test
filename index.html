Sunstone raised a new fund

Sunstone Partners closed its oversubscribed $925 million third fund, Sunstone Partners III (“Fund III”), at its hard cap. Fund III, which more than doubles the total committed capital managed by Sunstone Partners, is the firm’s largest fund to date and brings total committed LP capital to $1.7B since inception in 2015.

Our strategy will be consistent with prior funds – we will focus on growth equity and growth buyout investments in profitable technology enabled services and software companies, typically founder owned.

We will continue to focus on several sectors where we have deep expertise including cloud services, health care IT, cybersecurity and marketing technology.
